About

The Backlash Theory is a project about addressing racism in America through twenty posters. The word backlash is a robust opposing reaction to an idea, action, or object. The term applies to bias and discrimination against minoritized/marginalized groups. Examples include subtle approaches such as ridicule and condemnation or less subtle approaches like assassination and rape. For this project, our approach will be about ridiculing the white man.

Black people often sit back and take a peaceful approach when it comes to addressing wrongful racial treatment. The majority of blacks are taught from a young age to comply with white ideals. In this project, we want to ignore the peaceful approach and get whites upset and address wrongful racial treatment with brute force through designed posters.

Angelica Fields & Danille Simon
